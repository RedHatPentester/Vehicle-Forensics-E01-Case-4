# Vehicle-Forensics-E01-Case-4 - DOUBLE LIFE

A married senior bank official's wife reports the family vehicle missing every Thursday evening. The vehicle is never actually missing; 
the IVI shows it drives to the same East Legon address every Thursday at 19:00, stays 3 to 4 hours, and returns. 
The same female Bluetooth device pairs on every visit across 14 consecutive weeks. A secondary civil case is being built by the wife's lawyers. 
Teaches: Pattern of life analysis · recurring Bluetooth pairing · sensitive personal data handling · civil vs criminal forensics · data privacy

As an investigator, give us your findings and conclude if this is a double life.
